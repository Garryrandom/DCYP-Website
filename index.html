Current COVID-19 restrictions:

No Face to face

Our team will not host any face to face sessions with the exception of Detached or Outreach.

Virtual 1 to 1 available

There will be a number of virtual one to ones available on a weekly basis to access support and guidance.

Postal and drop of service for C-card service

We will offer a postal and drop off C-card service to young people aged 13-19 (including virtual sign ups).

Online group sessions

We are hosting online group sessions for our Youth Club members, Youth Council, people on Detached and those using our Sexual Health Support.

Regularly updated social media

Regular updates to social media pages with information on support, activities and signposting to young people.

Office is currently closed

Our office is closed as from today and all staff will work remotely from home.

Staff working from home

All staff are working from home and will be using online meetings to continue our work with young people and our partners.

Achievements

Sexual Health Bus

DCYP has been awarded the commission by Durham County Council for the Mobile Sexual Health Unit again, to visit areas in County Durham….call in for cuppa and chat it would be great to see you.

DCYP Deliveries

DCYP were fortunate enough to secure funding from Children In Need and Durham Area Action Partnership to make craft and food pack deliveries to 221 young people across County Durham.

Youth Council Christmas Deliveries

“Happy Christmas” to the people of Camsell Court. Each resident was given a gift and card from our Youth Council. This was made possible with funding from County Durham Community Foundation I Will.

Upshot

Durham AAP Covid Resilience funding and County Durham Community Foundation Covid 19 Sustainability funding has enabled DCYP to purchase a new database Upshot. This enables Durham City Youth Project to adhere to Covid secure procedures and provides greater flexibility when meeting young people on detached. Upshot records membership details, session outcomes which are used by funders and helps us to identify the areas of County Durham we are supporting with youth services.

Donate Digital

DCYP donated 8 laptops to the Donate Digital appeal which aims to end Digital Poverty by upgrading laptops and giving them to schools to give to children most in need.

Ambiton Quality Bronze Award

Durham city Youth Project was awarded the Ambition Quality Bronze mark. We were measured on 10 quality areas: NEEDS FINISHING

Inter-generational Events

Afternoon Tea, Pie, Peas Supper, Flower Arranging Demonstration and Christmas Lunch with the isolated and elderly of Framwellgate Moor. Our young volunteers from the Youth Council hosted these events... Thanks to County Durham Community Foundation and #1 Wil for making this possible!
